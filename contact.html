Common Questions

Quick answers to our most frequently asked questions

What are your delivery hours and areas?

We deliver Monday through Saturday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM. Our delivery area currently covers all major metropolitan areas within a 50-mile radius of our distribution centers. You can check if we deliver to your area by entering your zip code during checkout.

How do I place a bulk order?

You can place bulk orders through our dedicated bulk order form above, by calling our bulk order hotline at +1 (800) 555-4321, or by emailing sales@freshpicks.com. Our sales team will work with you to understand your requirements and provide a customized quote.

What is your freshness guarantee?

We guarantee that all our products are delivered at peak freshness. If you're not completely satisfied with the freshness of any item, contact us within 24 hours of delivery for a full refund or replacement. Our fruits and vegetables are sourced directly from farms and delivered within 24-48 hours of harvest.

Do you offer organic products?

Yes! We have a wide selection of certified organic fruits, vegetables, and juices. All organic products are clearly labeled on our website and packaging. Our organic produce comes from certified farms that meet USDA organic standards.
