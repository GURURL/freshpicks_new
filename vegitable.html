Our Story

FreshPicks was born from a simple idea: to deliver farm-fresh produce and healthy juices right to your doorstep while supporting local farmers and promoting sustainable agriculture.

Who We Are

Founded in 2015, FreshPicks is a leading ecommerce platform specializing in fresh fruits, vegetables, and cold-pressed juices. We're more than just a grocery delivery service - we're a movement towards healthier living and sustainable food systems.

Our team of nutritionists, farmers, and food enthusiasts work tirelessly to ensure that every product we deliver meets our high standards of quality, freshness, and nutritional value.

Our Process

At FreshPicks, we follow a farm-to-table approach that ensures maximum freshness and nutritional value in every product we deliver.

Our fruits and vegetables are hand-picked at peak ripeness and delivered within 24 hours of harvest. Our juices are cold-pressed to preserve nutrients and never contain added sugars or preservatives.

🌱

Organic Farming

We partner with certified organic farms that prioritize sustainable practices and avoid harmful pesticides.
